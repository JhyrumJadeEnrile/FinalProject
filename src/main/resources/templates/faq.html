Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

You can reset your password by navigating to the **Account Setting** page from the sidebar. You will find a "Change Password" section where you can enter your current password and set a new one.

Your current student roster is available under the **Students** link in the main navigation sidebar. This page lists all enrolled students with their basic information.

Yes, direct messaging is available. Click on the **Chat** link in the sidebar to access the messaging platform.

You can log out by clicking on your profile image in the top-right corner of the header. A dropdown menu will appear with a **Logout** button.
